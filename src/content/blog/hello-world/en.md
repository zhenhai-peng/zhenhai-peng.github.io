Every programmer's first program prints "Hello, World." It feels fitting that my first post does the same.

## Why I'm writing

I've been a developer for a few years now. During that time, I've consumed enormous amounts of written content — blog posts, documentation, technical papers, forum threads. All of it has shaped how I think and work.

Writing is how I think. When I have to articulate something clearly enough for someone else to understand, I often discover I didn't understand it as well as I thought. The blank page is an honest mirror.

So this blog is, first and foremost, for me. If it helps you too, that's a bonus I'll gladly take.

## What I'll write about

Mostly software. Sometimes the broader questions around it — how we collaborate, how we learn, what makes code *good*. Occasionally something completely unrelated.

I won't commit to a schedule. When I have something worth saying, I'll say it.

## A note on language

This site is bilingual — English and Chinese. Some posts will have translations, some won't. I'll try to translate the ones that feel worth the effort.

Thanks for being here. I hope something I write is useful to you.
